Resources for Queer Community

All resources on this page are what we call closed group resources. A closed group means that the resources are just for people who need them, because they are a part of that group.

These resources offer within-community peer supports, which means that they can connect you to people like you. They will have similar beliefs, culture, or experiences.

They also offer culturally informed care. This means that everyone has had training and experiences with the group, even if they are not a part of that group. The communities they work with have accepted them as safe. 

The Trevor Project

1 (866) 488-7386  (24/7)

The Trevor Project provides a 24/7 crisis and emotional support hotline for Queer individuals in need of support. They also provide education, community connection, advocacy materials, and more to the Queer community, especially Queer youth

Desi LGBTQ Helpline

1-908-367-3374 (hours may vary)

The Desi LGBTQ provides a hotline and online messaging support. Phone line operates between Thursdays and Sundays from 8-10 pm EST. They provide culturally competent services for South-Asian members of the LGBTQ community, and their friends
