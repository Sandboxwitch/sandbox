Resources for BIPOC

All resources on this page are closed group resources. A closed group means that the resources are just for people who need them, because they are a part of that group.

These resources offer within-community peer supports, which means that they can connect you to people like you. They will have similar beliefs, cultures, or experiences.

They also offer culturally informed care. This means that everyone has had training and experiences with the group, even if they are not a part of that group. The communities they work with have accepted them as safe.
