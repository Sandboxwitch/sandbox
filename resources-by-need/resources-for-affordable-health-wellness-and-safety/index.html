US National Local Services Referral Network 

Dial 211 from any US Phone, 24/7

The USA 211 network is different from state-to-state, but works to connect people to a variety of government assistance programs to help with the management of daily life. Similar to the emergency 911 line, the 211 line is a non-emergency service. It can be accessed by any phone at any time. Common assistance programs include low-cost clinics, sliding-scale therapists, housing, and food assistance programs
