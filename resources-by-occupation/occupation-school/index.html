Occupation: School

Whenever you are advocating within an institution, it is important to remember:

  1. The human resource and disability support staff of an institution exist for the betterment of that institution
  2. It is rare that these groups will prioritise the betterment of your health more than the comforts of the institution 
  3. It is recommended that people read up on rights and advocacy strategies before discussing your disability with an institution.
