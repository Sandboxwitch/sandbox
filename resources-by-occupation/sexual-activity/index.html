Sexual Activity

This page is a collection of educational media on sex, reproductive health, and intimacy for people with disabilities.

Many of the resources provided on this page are not written with trans, nonbinary, or intersex people in mind. The majority of sexual health information is uses outdated terminology based on a cisgender binary system. This webpage will be updated as better resources become available. In the meantime, the precautions section will identify if a resource was designed for a cisgender or a queer inclusive audience.Â
