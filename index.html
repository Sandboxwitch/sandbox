SANDbox

Self Advocacy Network for Disabilities

Build and plan your self-advovacy plan 

The goal of Sandbox is to connect Disabled folks to tools and information that can help them to self advocate and thrive. Self Advocacy can mean a lot of things. Sometimes we need to manage symptoms, sometimes we need to manage relationships, environments, hobbies, and even more.

Sandbox puts the focus on Disabled folks with actionable self-help resources, rather than information meant for people in caretaking roles. Everyone should have access to self help, empowerment, and peer support resources.
