Project Sleep

1 800 819 2043 (information hotline, hours may vary) 

Project Sleep is a nonprofit that works to raise awareness and understanding of the many types of sleep disorders that exist. They provide introductory-level fact sheets and information on various sleep disorders. They also have a support line where you can talk about your struggles with sleep disorders, and be connected to resources that could help

The Hypersomnia Foundation 

The Hypersomnia Foundation works to promote understanding and supports for people with hypersomnia sleep disorders. They have information on how to manage hypersomnia in relation to 

  • Education
  • Employment
  • Safe driving
  • Self advocacy for your health
  • Managing health insurance
  • And general day to day life

The Restless Leg Syndrome Foundation
The Restless Leg Syndrome Foundation aims to improve life experiences for people with restless leg syndrome. They provide informational guides to navigating healthcare, as well as support groups so people with restless leg syndrome can find support in each other

Wake Up Narcolepsy

Wake up Narcolepsy is an education and support hub with extensive resources for learning about and managing symptoms of narcolepsy, like:

  • Newly diagnosed to-do lists
  • Mental health resources
  • Narcolepsy related cardiovascular health resources
  • Sleep tracking advice
  • Sleep management toolkits
  • Education resources
  • Employment resources
  • And more
