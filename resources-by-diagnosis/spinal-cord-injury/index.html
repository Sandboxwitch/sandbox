The SCI Network and Open Source Wheelchairs
The SCI network project is a community made up of artists, craftsmen, and professionals with SCIs working to support each other. They partner with several charity organizations but also work to promote the hiring and services of people with SCI. 
Their Open Source Wheelchair project is highly unique. Craftsmen work to design safe, lightweight, and functional wheelchairs from common carpentry tools and materials available to the public. They post blueprints for various wheelchair formats with the known strengths and weaknesses of each model, as well as tips for how to build it.

Their designs can be used by anyone, but are especially made for people in places or situations without reliable access to prescription-quality wheelchairs

The Craig Institute
The Craig Institute is a global leader in research, healthcare, and advocacy for people with SCI, the Craig institute has many pages of resources on living with SCI. Topics range from critical health management, mobility, and parenting, to engaging with hobbies and enjoying positive community experiences.

The Christopher & Dana Reeve Foundation is nonprofit with a goal of improving life for people with SCI, and their loved ones. They offer a lot of resources on life with an SCI on an expansive variety of topics.
