Hadly Online Classes for Daily Living
Hadly is a free, online school for people adjusting to blindness or low vision. They offer classes on daily living, adjusting to vision loss, recreation, technology, Braille, and working

Blind Help Dot Net
This website is designed for tech savvy blind people by tech savvy blind people. It is best accessed with a screen reader, or other adaptive browsing tool. It is a global community run forum with advice on experiences, technology, access, culture, and far more.

The Lighthouse Guild

212 769 6318  (information support line, hours may vary) 

The Lighthouse Guild is a health services group that provides treatment and resources to people with low vision and blindness. They offer support services and recommendations  how to modify your home for safety, practice community mobility, and engage with tasks safely and independently. They also have a diverse range of phone support groups for people experiencing low vision and blindness with a variety of backgrounds and needs

The Focus Group

The Focus Group provides free, downloadable PDFs and videos on daily living and specific context supports for people with blindness or low vision.  Their library covers:

  • Eye care
  • Orientation
  • Safe mobility
  • Recreation
  • Child to adult transition ideas
  • Home modifications
  • And more
